About Me

My name is Ben Cart. I was born and raised in Southeast Georgia on St. Simons Island. I have had many interesting jobs and experiences since departing home. I have received a Bachelor's of Spanish from College of Charleston. I am a Certified Flight Instructor of instruments in helicopters as well as the holder of an Airline Transport Pilot certificate and have over 2,500 hours of fligth experience in everything from light helicopters to transport category jets.

Due to the coronavirus pandemic, I was furloughed from my position in the airlines. This has prompted me to finally chase after another dream career of mine: web development. I have now started a three month coding bootcamp in Charlotte, NC. I am excited to see what opportunities come from the hardwork put in during the bootcamp.
